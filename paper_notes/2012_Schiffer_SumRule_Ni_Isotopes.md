# Paper Notes: Schiffer et al. 2012 -- Test of Sum Rules in Nucleon Transfer

**Citation:** J.P. Schiffer, C.R. Hoffman, B.P. Kay et al., PRL 108, 022501 (2012)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevLett.108.022501
**Read:** 2026-04-25 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** neutron-adding (d,p), neutron-removal (p,d), proton-adding on four stable even Ni isotopes
- **Method:** DWBA analysis, absolute cross sections → spectroscopic factors → valence-orbit occupancies
- **Sum rule test:** Macfarlane-French sum rule -- occupancies from adding + removal should be consistent

## Key Result

Quantitative consistency of nucleon transfer as a probe of valence orbit occupancy demonstrated for Ni isotopes.
- SF from (d,p) + SF from (p,d) satisfy the Macfarlane-French sum rule (unitarity of spectroscopic amplitudes)
- This validates the DWBA framework for extracting reliable orbital occupancies from transfer reactions
