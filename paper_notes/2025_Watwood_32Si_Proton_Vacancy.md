## Physics

- **Reaction:** ³²Si(³He,d)³³P in inverse kinematics at 6.3 MeV/u (proton-removing)
- **Target:** ³²Si beam on (³He) target
- **States:** 1s-0d single-particle orbitals in ³³P up to ~4.5 MeV Ex
- **Method:** DWBA spectroscopic factors + complementary neutron-adding data → proton vacancies

## Key Results

- **Proton 1s₁/₂ in ³²Si and ³⁴Si:** consistent with **empty** (full vacancy)
- Proton 0d₅/₂ - 1s₁/₂ (Z=14) sub-shell closure explored across Si isotopes
- Neutron distribution: two neutron holes within N=20 gap (0d₃/₂ - 0f₇/₂)
- Si isotope chain (²⁸,³⁰,³²,³⁴Si): gradual changes in proton+neutron occupancy — no abrupt structure change
- Shell model in 1s-0d space describes ground-state distributions well

## Physics Significance

- ³²Si is N=18, Z=14 — near the N≈20 transition region; connection to island of inversion
- 34Si: ongoing discussions about 1p₃/₂-1p₁/₂ spin-orbit partner energies
- Confirms ³²Si mid-shell proton occupancy + nearly closed neutron occupancy within 1s-0d shell
- Complementary to ³⁰Si(t,p)³²Si two-neutron transfer (neutron case)
