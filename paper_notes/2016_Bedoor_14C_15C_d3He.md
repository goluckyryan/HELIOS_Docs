# Paper Notes: Bedoor et al. 2016 -- 14,15C(d,3He)13,14B Proton-Removing Reactions

**Citation:** S. Bedoor, A.H. Wuosmaa, M. Albers et al., Phys.Rev.C 93, 044323 (2016)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.93.044323
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ¹⁴C(d,³He)¹³B and ¹⁵C(d,³He)¹⁴B in inverse kinematics
- **Type:** Proton-removing reaction -- probes proton occupancy of ¹⁴,¹⁵C ground states
- **Products:** ³He analyzed by HELIOS, final states in ¹³,¹⁴B
- **Method:** Angular distributions → spectroscopic information about ¹³,¹⁴B states

## Key Result

- Proton occupancy of ¹⁴,¹⁵C measured via (d,³He)
- Angular distributions for transitions from both reactions obtained
- Demonstrates HELIOS versatility: **(d,³He) proton-removal** in addition to (d,p), (d,α)

## Significance

- HELIOS reaction modes: (d,p), (p,d), (d,α), (d,³He), (d,t), (p,α) -- versatile instrument
- Proton-removal complements neutron-adding for complete occupancy picture
- Same light p/sd-shell nuclei as Back 2010, Bedoor 2013 B/C series
