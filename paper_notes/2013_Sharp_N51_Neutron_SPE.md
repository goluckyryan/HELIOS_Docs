# Paper Notes: Sharp et al. 2013 -- Neutron SPE Outside N=50 Core

**Citation:** D.K. Sharp, B.P. Kay, J.S. Thomas, S.J. Freeman, J.P. Schiffer et al., Phys.Rev.C 87, 014312 (2013)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.87.014312
**Read:** 2026-04-26

## Physics

- **Reactions:** (d,p) and (α,³He) on ⁸⁸Sr, ⁹⁰Zr, ⁹²Mo (conventional spectrometer) + ²H(⁸⁶Kr,p) at 10 MeV/u (HELIOS)
- **Goal:** Single-neutron properties of N=51 nuclei outside N=50 shell closure
- **Mixed approach:** Conventional + HELIOS measurements on different isotopes

## Key Result

- N=51 neutron SPE systematics across Z=38-42 established
- ⁸⁶Kr(d,p) at HELIOS provides inverse-kinematics data point
- Shell evolution from N=50 to N=52 mapped
