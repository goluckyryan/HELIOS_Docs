# Paper Notes: Chen et al. 2018 -- Effective NN Interaction via 21F(d,p)22F

**Citation:** J. Chen, C.R. Hoffman, T. Ahn et al., Phys.Rev.C 98, 014325 (2018)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.98.014325
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ²¹F(d,p)²²F in inverse kinematics (radioactive ²¹F beam at HELIOS)
- **Goal:** Determine effective nucleon-nucleon interaction via single-neutron configurations
- **States observed:** π0d₅/₂ coupled to ν0d₅/₂, ν1s₁/₂, ν0d₃/₂ up to ~5 MeV Ex
- **Method:** DWBA angular distributions → spectroscopic factors

## Key Result

- Single-neutron configurations of low-lying ²²F states determined
- Spectroscopic factors measured for sd-shell orbitals (d₅/₂, s₁/₂, d₃/₂)
- Probes effective NN interaction in the sd shell through multiplet energies and SFs
- J. Chen (now at SUSTech) is corresponding author -- also co-author on Hoffman 2026 (h096 predecessor)

## Relevance to h096

- Same sd-shell orbitals: 0d₅/₂, 1s₁/₂, 0d₃/₂ -- same as the 32Si ground-state configurations
- Demonstrates HELIOS capability with F isotope radioactive beams
- DWBA method and SF extraction directly analogous to h096 approach
- Studies proton-neutron multiplet structure -- relevant to understanding 32Si sd/pf mixing
