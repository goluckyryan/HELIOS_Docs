# Paper Notes: Wuosmaa et al. 2014 -- Stretched States via (d,α) Reaction

**Citation:** A.H. Wuosmaa, J.P. Schiffer, S. Bedoor et al., Phys.Rev.C 90, 061301(R) (2014)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.90.061301
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ¹²C(d,α)¹²B and ¹³C(d,α)¹²B (removing proton+neutron pair)
- **Selectivity:** (d,α) preferentially populates "stretched" states where J=2j (max spin)
  - Neutron and proton aligned → specific two-body matrix element
- **Goal:** Probe two-nucleon overlap functions (vs single-nucleon in (d,p))

## Key Result

- Stretched states identified in ¹²B and ¹³B
- Two-body matrix elements extracted from cross sections
- Demonstrates HELIOS capability for (d,α) two-nucleon transfer reactions
- Wuosmaa also co-authored the first HELIOS paper (Back 2010, ¹²B)

## Relevance

- Shows HELIOS versatility: not just (d,p) and (p,d) but also (d,α)
- (d,α) selectivity for J=2j states is complementary to (d,p) SF measurements
- Could be applied in future experiments to probe two-body correlations
