# Paper Notes: Hoffman et al. 2012 -- 19O(d,p)20O at HELIOS

**Citation:** C.R. Hoffman, B.B. Back, B.P. Kay, J.P. Schiffer et al., Phys.Rev.C 85, 054318 (2012)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.85.054318
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ¹⁹O(d,p)²⁰O in inverse kinematics (neutron-adding)
- **Q-value resolution:** ~175 keV FWHM
- **States:** 8 states populated with measurable strength
- **Orbitals:** 0d₅/₂, 1s₁/₂, 0d₃/₂ -- **same sd-shell as h096 (³²Si)**
- **Method:** DWBA angular distributions → spectroscopic factors → T=1 two-body matrix elements

## Key Result

- Full spectroscopic study of sd-shell ²⁰O states
- Two-body matrix elements deduced and compared to shell-model
- Demonstrates 175 keV Q-value resolution achievable with HELIOS at this mass

## Relevance to h096

- **Direct prototype** for h096 ³¹Si(d,p)³²Si analysis
- Same sd-shell orbitals (0d₅/₂, 1s₁/₂, 0d₃/₂) -- same L=2 transfers
- Same team: Hoffman, Back, Kay, Schiffer (all h096/Hoffman 2026 co-authors)
- Shows how HELIOS measures absolute cross sections and extracts SFs
