# Paper Notes: Wuosmaa et al. 2010 -- 15C(d,p)16C Exotic Behavior

**Citation:** A.H. Wuosmaa, B.B. Back, S. Baker, B.A. Brown, C.M. Deibel, P. Fallon et al., PRL 105, 132501 (2010)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevLett.105.132501
**Read:** 2026-04-26

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ¹⁵C(d,p)¹⁶C in inverse kinematics at HELIOS
- **Nucleus:** ¹⁶C, N=10, near drip line
- **Prior puzzle:** EM transition rates suggested exotic valence neutron decoupling from core
- **Result:** Transfer SFs + shell-model calculations reconcile both transfer AND EM data

## Significance

- **Paper #1 in the HELIOS publication list** -- first published HELIOS science result (PRL 2010)
- Shows HELIOS can probe exotic nuclei and resolve longstanding puzzles
- Wuosmaa is HELIOS co-developer (also Lighthall 2010 commissioning)
