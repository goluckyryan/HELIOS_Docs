# Paper Notes: Kay et al. 2013 -- 130Te Neutron Occupancies for 0νββ

**Citation:** B.P. Kay, T. Bloxham, S.A. McAllister, J.A. Clark, C.M. Deibel et al., Phys.Rev.C 87, 011302(R) (2013)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.87.011302
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** Single-neutron adding + removing on ¹²⁸,¹³⁰Te and ¹³⁰,¹³²Xe
- **Goal:** Constrain nuclear matrix elements for ¹³⁰Te → ¹³⁰Ba 0νββ decay
- **Orbitals probed:** 0g₇/₂, 1d₅/₂, 1d₃/₂, 2s₁/₂, 0h₁₁/₂ (gdsh shell)
- **Method:** Self-consistent SF determination, sum rule constraints

## Key Result

- Change in orbital vacancies between ¹³⁰Te and ¹³⁰Xe ground states measured
- Constrains the 0νββ nuclear matrix element (NME) uncertainty
- Part of same program as Xe-136 occupancies (Kay 2011)
- Rapid Communication -- high-impact result

## Context

- Companion to Kay 2011 (¹³⁶Xe) and Szwec 2016 (¹³⁶Xe full paper)
- Same technique: transfer reactions probe valence orbit vacancies → 0νββ NME
- Not HELIOS specifically (conventional spectrometer measurements)
