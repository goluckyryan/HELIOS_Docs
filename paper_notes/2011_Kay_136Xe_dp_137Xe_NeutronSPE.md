# Paper Notes: Kay et al. 2011 -- 136Xe(d,p)137Xe Single-Neutron Energies

**Citation:** B.P. Kay, J.P. Schiffer, S.J. Freeman, C.R. Hoffman et al., Phys.Rev.C 84, 024325 (2011)
**DOI:** 10.1103/PhysRevC.84.024325
**Read:** 2026-04-26 (from HELIOS_Publications_Summary)

---

## Physics

- **Reaction:** ¹³⁶Xe(d,p)¹³⁷Xe in inverse kinematics at **10 MeV/u** (same as h096)
- **Resolution:** ~100 keV excitation energy (better than h096 ~300 keV)
- **Goal:** Single-neutron properties of N=83 nucleus ¹³⁷Xe
- **Motivation:** 0νββ decay of ¹³⁶Xe → ¹³⁶Ba -- neutron occupancies affect nuclear matrix elements

## Key Result

- Complete single-neutron strength distribution in ¹³⁷Xe determined
- Absolute cross sections + angular distributions + spectroscopic factors
- Connects to Swimec 2016 (already noted): valence neutron occupancies for 0νββ

## Relevance

- **Same beam energy** (10 MeV/u) as h096 -- directly comparable kinematics
- Better resolution than h096 (100 vs 300 keV) -- heavier beam → less kinematic compression
- Demonstrates HELIOS capability for N~83 shell closure region
- Precursor paper to the full 0νββ occupancy program (Szwec 2016, Freeman 2017)
